Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

🐾 How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days within Gaborone. For areas outside Gaborone, please allow 3-5 business days. You'll receive a tracking link once your order ships.

🐾 Can I return or exchange products?

Yes! We offer a 14-day return policy for unopened products. If your pet doesn't like the food, we'll help you find an alternative. Contact our support team for assistance.

🐾 Do you offer bulk discounts?

Absolutely! For bulk orders of 5+ bags, contact our wholesale team at wholesale@happybeasts.co.bw for special pricing. We also offer subscribe & save options for regular customers.

🐾 Are your products vet-approved?

Yes! All our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers with veterinary nutritionists on their teams. Many of our brands are recommended by local veterinarians.
